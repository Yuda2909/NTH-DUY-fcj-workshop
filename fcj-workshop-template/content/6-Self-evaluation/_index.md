During my internship at **Công ty TNHH Amazon Web Services Viet Nam** from **17/04/2026** to **10/07/2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply my academic knowledge in a real working and cloud learning environment.

Throughout the internship, I participated in the **Workforce Bootcamp - First Cloud AI Journey** program. I learned about AWS cloud services, completed hands-on labs, attended company events, worked directly at the company office, and participated in the research and development of the **AWS BILLO** project, an e-wallet system combined with merchant POS and store management.

Through this internship, I improved my understanding of cloud computing, AWS services, system design, serverless architecture, technical documentation, reporting, and project implementation. I also became more aware of the importance of responsibility, self-learning, communication, and teamwork in a professional environment.

In terms of work ethic, I tried to complete assigned tasks, follow the internship schedule, and actively learn from mentors, documents, and team discussions. Although I still have limitations, especially in troubleshooting and teamwork experience, I believe this internship helped me improve both technical and professional skills.

### Strengths

- I was willing to learn new AWS services and cloud computing concepts.
- I actively followed the internship learning roadmap and completed assigned worklogs.
- I improved my ability to read technical documents and summarize AWS blog content.
- I gained practical experience with AWS services such as IAM, VPC, EC2, S3, DynamoDB, Lambda, API Gateway, Cognito, and CloudWatch.
- I became more familiar with serverless architecture and how AWS services can be combined in a real project.
- I maintained a responsible attitude toward assigned tasks and internship requirements.

### Needs Improvement

- I need to improve my troubleshooting skills, especially when working with AWS services, backend errors, deployment issues, and CloudWatch Logs.
- I need to become more confident when analyzing errors, identifying root causes, and proposing suitable solutions.
- I need to improve my teamwork skills, especially in task coordination, communication, and sharing progress with team members.
- I should participate more actively in group discussions and ask questions earlier when facing technical issues.
- I need to continue improving my ability to explain technical ideas clearly and concisely.

### Overall Reflection

Overall, this internship was a valuable learning experience for me. It helped me understand how cloud computing knowledge can be applied to real projects and how important AWS services are in building modern applications. Although I still need to improve my problem-solving and teamwork skills, I gained more confidence in learning new technologies, working with cloud services, and participating in a project development process.

After this internship, I will continue strengthening my AWS knowledge, practicing more hands-on labs, improving my debugging skills, and developing better teamwork habits for future projects.
