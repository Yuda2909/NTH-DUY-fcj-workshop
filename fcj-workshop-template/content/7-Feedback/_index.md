This section presents my personal sharing and feedback after participating in the **Workforce Bootcamp - First Cloud AI Journey** program. Overall, the program gave me a valuable opportunity to learn about AWS services, cloud computing, hands-on labs, teamwork, and real project development. Besides the positive experiences, I would also like to provide some constructive feedback to help improve the program for future interns.

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**
The working environment was friendly, open, and supportive. During the internship, I had the opportunity to learn from mentors, company members, and other participants. The program helped me become more familiar with a professional cloud learning environment and encouraged me to explore AWS services through both self-study and hands-on practice.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**
The mentor and team admin supported interns throughout the program by providing guidance, learning materials, and important information. When I faced difficulties during AWS labs or project development, I could ask questions and receive useful suggestions. This helped me improve my self-learning ability and technical problem-solving mindset.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**
The internship content was highly relevant to my major in Information Technology. The tasks involved cloud computing, system design, backend services, serverless architecture, database design, API integration, and project documentation. These topics helped me connect what I learned at university with real-world technology and cloud implementation.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**
During the internship, I learned many new skills, including AWS service usage, reading technical documentation, writing worklogs, summarizing AWS blog posts, designing system architecture, and developing a project based on AWS services. I also improved my awareness of cost optimization, security, monitoring, and deployment in a cloud environment.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**
The company culture and program spirit were positive. The program encouraged learning, sharing, and collaboration among interns. Participating in company events and direct office working sessions also helped me better understand the professional working environment and improve my confidence when communicating with others.

**6. Internship Management and Communication**
In my opinion, one point that could be improved is group communication and information management. Sometimes, important announcements could be mixed with daily discussions, making them easy to miss or difficult to find again later.

To improve this, I suggest creating two separate communication groups:

- One group for daily discussion, questions, teamwork, and general communication.
- One group only for important announcements, deadlines, schedules, documents, and official updates.

This separation would help interns follow important information more easily and reduce the chance of missing key announcements.

---

### Additional Questions

**What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**
The most satisfying part of my internship was the opportunity to learn AWS services through both theory and practice. I could start from basic AWS concepts and gradually move toward system design and project implementation. I also appreciated the chance to participate in events and work directly at the company office.

**What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**
I think the company should improve group communication management. Important announcements should be separated from general discussion messages so that interns can track deadlines, schedules, and official information more easily.

**If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**
Yes, I would recommend this internship program to friends who are interested in cloud computing, AWS, and real project development. The program provides useful learning materials, hands-on experience, and opportunities to understand how cloud services are applied in practice.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**
I suggest organizing communication channels more clearly. A separate announcement group should be used for important information, while another group can be used for discussion and questions. This would make the program easier to follow and help interns manage information more effectively.

**Would you like to continue this program in the future?**
Yes. If I have the opportunity, I would like to continue joining similar programs in the future to improve my AWS knowledge, cloud project experience, and professional working skills.

**Any other comments:**
Overall, I am grateful for the opportunity to participate in the First Cloud AI Journey program. The internship helped me gain more knowledge, practical experience, and motivation to continue learning cloud computing and AWS technologies.
